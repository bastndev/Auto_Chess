Online Chess That Knows Nothing About You

chesslablab.com allows to learn and play chess online without being tracked at all. It doesn't know anything about you. This is made possible because it does not use cookies of any sort and does not store information as other apps do, not even nicknames or chess games.

  • Zero cookies.
  • Zero usernames, emails and passwords.
  • Zero personal data.
  • Zero tracking of chess games.
  • Zero behavioral data.

ChesslaBlab is open-source software and can be used and extended by developers all over the world to create amazing chess web apps.
